### Core features of an application

1. Ability to manage teachers
2. Teachers hold courses
3. Students are put into groups to attend courses with specific teacher
4. Courses have prices
5. Courses are paid monthly (invoices should be generated monthly)


### Dashboards for cockpit view

1. How many students are enrolled in any courses
2. How many active courses there are
3. Per group, how many students have paid their monthly invoice 
4. Outstanding payments
